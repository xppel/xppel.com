Ripple is a concept for a handheld PDA (personal digital assistant) designed to provide better support for retail employees and managers in daily store operations.

Focusing on inventory management, pricing, customer assistance, and task coordination, the design emphasizes single-handed usability, intuitive navigation, and ergonomic efficiency. It also includes stereoscopic LiDAR sensors for spatial tracking and more context-aware store workflows.

The solution streamlines workflows to reduce physical and cognitive strain in fast-paced retail environments.
